2024

In February 2024, while working for INTERalliance, I helped execute TechOlympics 2024: Empower, at our brand new venue P&G Towers in Cincinnati. I helped with on-site tech support and registration, and ran a Wiki Races competition and an Internet of Things workshop. Before the event, I updated the website to reflect the upcoming event.

In January 2024, while working for INTERalliance, released brand new versions of the INTERalliance.org and TechOlympics.org websites powered by Jekyll and GitHub Pages to replaces the Wordpress versions. This change finally brought of all INTERalliance's websites back in house.

2023

In March 2023, as Vice President of the Norse IoT Club, I designed a Website Workshop event to teach NKU students basic HTML so they could showcase their skills to employers with a personal resume website. I created a guide that covered connecting to NKU's network drives, six HTML templates, and an index site that used JavaScript to allow students to pick a template with dynamic CSS.

Also in March, I released Tier Away! I started working on it in the summer of the previous year as a hobby project to learn more about JavaScript by developing a client-side tier list designer app. I had used a few other sites like it over the years, but none of them did the job.

In February 2023, while working for INTERalliance, I helped execute TechOlympics 2023: Electrify, the first TechOlympics back in person since the pandemic. I helped with on-site tech support and registration, and before the event helped update the website to reflect the upcoming event.

2022

In November 2022, I became Vice President of the Norse IoT Club at NKU.

In August 2022, I was hired on to NKU's Informatics+ center to help with day to day operations and event planning and execution.

In Summer 2022, I led a five person team to completely redesign INTERalliance.org from the ground up, improving elements ignored over the years. Our team started from a blank Wordpress site and based on the designs created by our graphic designer, created a new INTERalliance.org in Elementor, a WYSISWYG editor for Wordpress. The new site also employs dynamic data for the ILC, Board, and Home pages that can be added to without having to edit the site manually.

In February 2022, I helped plan and execute TechOlympics 2022: Byte Back, and its accompanying Hackathon. On the day of the event, I helped with Zoom Administration and Whova Moderation. Before the event, I was in charge of updating the website to reflect the event.

2021

In November 2021, I helped plan and execute the ZeroDay/INTERalliance Extra-Life Game Day charity livestream. I designed a website on INTERalliance.org for the event and stayed up administrating the event and also streaming to support the ExtraLife charity.

In Summer 2021, I rebuilt the website for the Carnegie, a local nonprofit, as the sole frontend developer based off design file mockups they created.

In February 2021, I helped plan and execute TechOlympics 2021: Illuminate, the first ever virtual TechOlympics. Throughout the month log event, I helped with Zoom Administration and Whova Moderation. Before the event, I was in charge of updating the website to reflect the event.

2020

In November of 2020, I helped with Zoom Administration for the annual WIiT conference.

In August of 2020, I started at Northern Kentucky University, studying Computer Information Technology.

In February 2020, I helped plan and execute TechOlympics 2020: Activate, my first TechOlympics as an organizer and the first TechOlympics at the Great American Ball Park. Before the event, I helped set up the website and Whova for the new event.

2019

Over the summer of June 2019, I worked on archiving and uploading old competition and event materials from previous years. I also familiarized myself with the INTERalliance website and Wordpress as a whole, and would begin updating the site when necessary, adding announcements, new pages, and updating anything that would become out of date.

In June 2019, I was hired at INTERalliance as the Internal Systems Intern.
